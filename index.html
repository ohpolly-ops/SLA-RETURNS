How this works
  • Name popup: we save your name locally and greet you. Use “Change name” to update.
  • Unpacking: PPH default is 70. Required hours = returns ÷ PPH. Capacity comes from 6h / 9h / 11h staff counts (effective hours 5.667 / 8.25 / 11).
  • SLA: Age (h) since Received at. Time Left = SLA − Age. Status colours by time left.
  • Data: Everything saves in your browser (localStorage). Use CSV/JSON to back up or move data.
